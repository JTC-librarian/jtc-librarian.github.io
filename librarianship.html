The Format and Display of the MARC 505 Contents Note: Some Recent practical Developments

Clark, James. 2024. The Format and Display of the MARC 505 Contents Note: Some Recent Practical Developments. Cataloging and Classification Quarterly.

This article identifies trends in the use of the MARC 505 fields through the analysis of records downloaded from the UK’s National Bibliographic Knowledgebase. As well as an increasing use of the 505 field, it is noted that an increasing proportion of records do not use the long-established format of delimiting items with a double hyphen, but that instead there is a rapid increase in the proportion of records that contain HTML formatting in this field. A project at the author’s library demonstrates that modern public catalogs are capable of sophisticated display without requiring HTML in underlying records.

Access via publisher (preferred). Open Access to electronic off print.
